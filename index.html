TRAVEL TO THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines

The Philippines is a country that has over 7,000 tropical islands.The Philippines is divided into three groups: Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao. It is full of many beautiful beaches and hidden majestic forests.

Reasons Why You Should Visit the Philippines

  • To discover new places;
  • To challenge yourself;
  • Learn new skills and test your abilities;
  • Meet new people; and
  • Relax and have fun.

Top 3 Places to Visit in the Philippines

  1. Boracay

    2. Boracay Island is is known as the "Capital Beach of the Philippines" beacause there are more than 12 beaches in the Philippines. This small island is found the west Visayas Region. White Beach is one of the most popular beaches and I recommened that you should come.

  2. Bohol

    3. Bohol Island is found in the Philippines's Central Visayas Region. It shows the natural beauty of the country. One of the most unique place in Bohol is the Chocolate Hills in Carmen. During dry season, the hills turn brown and resembles the famous chocolate, Kisses.

  3. Cebu

    4. The island of Cebu, one of the best diving and snorkeling in the Philippines. If you love exploring the under-world, then Cebu is one of the best place for you. You can see whale sharks, coral reefs, and sea turtles.
